Caustic Concentration Calculator

This tool helps calculate the proper preparation of caustic soda for our bottle washer machines. Excessive use doesn't improve cleaning and may lead to waste. The formula below ensures optimal performance:

\[ C_1V_1 + C_aV_a = C_fV_f \]

Where:
C1 ‚Üí Initial concentration
V1 ‚Üí Initial volume
Ca ‚Üí Additional concentration
Va ‚Üí Additional volume
Cf ‚Üí Final concentration
Vf ‚Üí Final volume

To calculate additional volume:

\[ V_a = \frac{C_f V_1 - C_1 V_1}{C_a} \]
